    About Dream Live Foundation

    Dream Live Foundation is a non-profit organization founded by our CEO Sabelo Wiseman Dlamini, dedicated to improving lives across Africa. We encourage dreamers and support young people in bridging the gap between dreams and reality.

    Boomerang Hub Graduate Center

    A South African youth-led graduates nonprofit company connecting and empowering disadvantaged & disabled graduates from rural areas and townships. We provide future-ready training programs and create pathways to employment opportunities.

    Community Development

    An initiative defining the future families, workforce and communities of South Africa through sustainable development programs.

    Makerspaces

    Workshops offering materials, tools, components, machines, and training to makers, supporting the development of practical skills across Africa.

    Feed a Child

    A school feeding program focusing on improving nutrition and health status for less fortunate children.

    Food Donations & Backyard Farming

    Combining immediate relief through food parcels with sustainable solutions through backyard farming education, addressing food security challenges in South Africa.

    Dream Green Africa

    A community-based initiative promoting renewable energy production and clean energy solutions for rural communities and farms in South Africa.

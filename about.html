Our Roots

Founded in mid-summer of 2015, Dreamcelsius Group (Pty) Ltd began as a small trading company with a vision that would grow to transform African agriculture.

Today, we stand as a diversified Pan-African investment powerhouse, with deep roots in agriculture, agribusiness, and international trade. As South Africa's second-largest exporter of citrus fruits, we're proud to share our nation's bounty with the world.

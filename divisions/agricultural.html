Our Mission

The Group is fully black owned with the focus on growing, packing, distribution, sourcing, marketing and export of high-grade agricultural produce worldwide. We are responsible for farming and export operations connecting farmers to consumers in different international markets.

Food production must be more than double to feed the growing population and production need to increase using the resources available. We work hand in hand with local farmers, producers, silo owners, traders and suppliers to deliver high quality agricultural products.

Growth & Investment

In South Africa the country's population is growing almost 2% per year, expected to reach 82 million by 2035. The group is investing in 80,000 hectares of farmland across the country for Commercial farming and agricultural production.
